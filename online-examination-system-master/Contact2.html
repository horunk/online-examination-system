FAQ Questions:


  1. When will I receive my exam results?

    2. Your score becomes official once The Smart Technology People Organization (STPO) approves it. You will be notified by email typically within 24 to 48 hours after you complete your exam. However, if STPO determines that your exam results require further analysis, there may be a delay in receiving your official score, and you will be notified. The analysis may take up to 45 days to complete. Additionally, an officially published score may be invalidated for misconduct, score anomalies, or breaches of The STPO’s Code of Ethics.

  2. How is the exam scored?

    3. Each exam question that contributes to your score has been pretested to ensure statistical validity. Your raw score is calculated based on the number of questions answered correctly and is converted to a reporting scale ranging from 250 to 750 points. A score of 600 or higher is required to pass each exam; however, if you passed the exam, your score report will indicate just that — no numeric score will be reported.

  3. I recently achieved certification status. How do I get my certificate?

    4. Once you complete all the requirements to become certified, you will receive a confirmation e-mail. This e-mail will contain instructions directing you to STPO, where you will request your certificate. Your certificate will be printed and mailed to the address designated. Please take this time to be sure that your contact information, mailing address, and name as you wish for it to appear on your certificate are correct.

  4. When I can retake exam?

    5. If you failed your exam or exam part, you must wait at least 90 days before you can retake the exam.
